About Us

We started selling record players in our garage.

Our Story

We started out as a small group of music enthusiasts who were frustrated with the lack of high-quality record players on the market. So we decided to take matters into our own hands and started building our own record players in our garage.

As word of our high-quality products spread, we began to receive more and more orders from music lovers all over the world. Today, we're proud to be one of the leading record player brands in the world, and we're still committed to delivering the highest-quality products to our customers.".

Our Mission

At our core, we believe that music has the power to bring people together and create meaningful connections. That's why our mission is to provide our customers with the best possible listening experience, whether they're listening to their favorite albums or discovering new music.

We're constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible in record player design and technology, and we're committed to creating products that not only sound great, but look great too. Above all, we're passionate about music and we're dedicated to sharing that passion with our customers.
